About Me

UI / UX Designer

Hi! I’m Julie, a Brazilian UX/UI designer with +10 years of experience currently living in Spain. I started my early career as a Graphic Designer in Brazil. A couple years later, I moved to Argentina where I could start working with UI Design and learn more about UX. I also had the opportunity to live and work in Poland for almost 4 years, working as a UX/UI Designer consultant for B2B/B2C ecommerce projects on different platforms. From 2021 I worked as an UX/UI Designer on a mobile app at a startup company in the healthcare sector based in the Netherlands.


I am an Adventurer , constantly looking to improve my design skills and deliver not only beautiful, but also functional designs. I love design thinking as a methodology to solve usability problems with innovative ideas and I’m always trying to put myself in the user's shoes. I’m a methodical and curious person, passionate about art, photography, traveling and everything that can inspire me in general.
